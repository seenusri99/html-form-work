ORTANT REMINDERS: uant to Section 124.2 and Section 145 of R.A. 8293 The applicant or the registrant shall file DECLARATION OF ACTUAL USE (DAU ) of the mark with evidence to that effect, as prescribed by the Regulations within three (3) years from the filing date of application. Otherwise, the application shall be REFUSED or the mark shall be REMOVED from the Register by the Director. A certificate of registration shall remain in force for ten (10) years; provided, that the registrant shall file a DECLARATION OF ACTUAL USE to that effect, or shall show valid reasons based on the existence of obstacles to such use, as prescribed by the Regulations, within one (1) year from the fifth (5 th ) anniversary of the date of registration of the mark. Otherwise, the mark shall be REMOVED from the Register by the Office
